Mobile Massage Therapist

ABOUT ME

I am a professional, reliable massage therapist who prides on caring for others and aims to deliver a service that is of a high standard. I regularly attend refresher sessions and courses to improve my knowledge and skills.

I trained and qualified as an Holistic massage therapist at the Bristol College of Massage and Bodywork, I attained a level 4 certificate and a first aid certificate.

Since I graduated I have set up a mobile massage business providing massages to people who live in Wellington/Taunton and surrounding areas. I am a registered member of the Massage Training Institute (MTI) and fully insured by Balens.

BENEFITS OF A REGULAR MASSAGE


  • Relieves muscle tension and the aches and pains that come with it.
  • Improves joint function, flexibility and movement.
  • Increases blood and lymph flow around the body.
  • Reduces the risk of headaches and improves migraines.
  • Reduces and anxiety and stress levels through everyday life by helping your body and mind relax.
  • Helps you connect with your body and mind
  • Makes you feel good

WHAT I OFFER


Rest and Repair offers a tailor-made massage individual to each client's needs and requirements. Being trained in Holistic massage means that I can massage a wide variety of people and address any specific requirements they may have.

I am also trained to massage pregnant women from 3months right up to birth. There are some medical conditions where I would require medical confirmation before an appointment.
