ABOUT ME

I was born in 1986 in Taunton, Somerset where I was raised, I now live in Wellington with a family of my own.

Wanting to help people is something that has always been a desire of mine; which I very much enjoy, I started my working life as a nursery nurse and then looking after the elderly and vulnerable enabling them to stay in their own homes.

I joined the Bristol College of Massage & Bodywork as a student and studied an Holistic massage course where I qualified as a level 4 holistic massage therapist and attained a first aid certificate. This qualification also means that I am MTI registered (Massage Training Institute).

Since then I have attended two training courses-

  • Mobilising Massage (movement of joints)
  • Pregnancy Massage
I am keen to attend more massage courses to further my knowledge and offer other therapies in the future, watch this space!

MY QUALIFICATIONS


what they mean to you

Level 4 holistic massage therapist enables me to practice Holistic massage as a professional. Looks at your body as a whole and with a consultation, having a chat about you and how you live your life, we set out at tailoring a massage that benefits "YOU".

Mobilising massage means that I can move joints with care and easy for my clients and also to prevent me from injuring myself by showing me the correct way to posturally position my self.

Why would you want this as part of a massage?

Well;

- It Helps build up the cartilage in joint particularly helping those who have joint complains.
- It also helps to free your joints that have been held in place by daily tension/stress etc coaxing them back into their natural position.
- Mobilisation also is another way of freeing up tendons that attach muscle to bone.

Preganancy massage

Spend 1hr or 1.5hrs relaxing with a massage which can benefit your mind and body (and baby quite likes it too!)
The benefit on your body...

- Relieves pain and tension as your baby grows.
- Relieves anxiety and stress.
- Reducing stress can help improve the digestive system.
- Boost blood and lymph circulation around the body.
- Helps release Oxytocin and Endorphins which can help discomfort and pain. Particularly those who suffer from SPD and PGP.
- Helps reduce stretch marks by working in towards your belly from your sides.
